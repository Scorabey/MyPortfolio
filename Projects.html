Learnly

A lesson-based app for on-demand skill-building.

UI/UX Branding Desktop
Role
UI/UX Designer Researcher
Context
As a UI/UX Designer and researcher for Learnly, an educational platform designed to enhance interactive learning experiences, my role was integral to creating user-centric solutions. I collaborated with a cross-functional team to understand the needs of students and educators, conducting usability studies and interviews to identify pain points and opportunities for improvement. Using these insights, I designed intuitive interfaces that simplified navigation and improved engagement, ensuring the platform catered to diverse learning styles.
